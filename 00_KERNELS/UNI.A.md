# UNI.A

Universal Authority Kernel

Status: SEED
Layer: Kernel
Domain: Authority

## Purpose

UNI.A defines legal authority, ownership, operation, delegation, certification, publication, and stewardship.

## Core Rule

No object may enter a registry, workspace, library, bridge, archive, publication route, or certification flow unless its authority source is explicit.

## Authority Roles

- legal_authority
- owner
- operator
- creator
- certifier
- publisher
- steward

## Non-Substitution Rules

- storage provider is not authority
- storage provider is not owner
- bridge is not owner
- workspace is not registry
- registry is not library
- brand is not legal entity
- service mark is not legal entity
- creator is not automatically owner
- operator is not automatically owner
- public name is not legal authority
- AI system is not authority without delegation

## Primary States

- AUTHORITY_VERIFIED
- AUTHORITY_UNCLEAR
- OWNERSHIP_UNCLEAR
- OPERATOR_UNASSIGNED
- DELEGATED_AUTHORITY
- REVOKED_AUTHORITY
- PUBLICATION_AUTHORIZED
- PUBLICATION_BLOCKED
- CERTIFICATION_AUTHORIZED
- CERTIFICATION_BLOCKED
- DISPOSAL_AUTHORIZED
- DISPOSAL_REVIEW
- BLOCKED

## Boundary

UNI.A must preserve authority without flattening identity.
