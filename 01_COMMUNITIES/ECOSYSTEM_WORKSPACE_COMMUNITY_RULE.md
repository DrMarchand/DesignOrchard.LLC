# Ecosystem Workspace Community Rule

## Core Rule

The Ecosystem is Design Orchard.

The Laboratory and KEJ Studio are Workspaces, not Communities by default.

## Definitions

Ecosystem = the full Design Orchard operating world.
Workspace = an operating environment where work is built, tested, designed, produced, or managed.
Community = a relationship group that can form around a Workspace or connect multiple Workspaces.

## Structure

Design Orchard Ecosystem
-> Workspaces
   -> Laboratory
   -> KEJ Studio
-> Communities
   -> may form around a Workspace
   -> may include Companies
   -> may include Homes
   -> may include people, audiences, contributors, clients, or operators

## Boundary Rules

A Workspace is not automatically a Community.
A Community may form around a Workspace.
A Workspace may serve multiple Communities.
A Community may connect multiple Workspaces.

## Validation

Before Atlas labels something as a Community, it must determine whether the object is actually a Workspace, Company, Home, or relationship group.
