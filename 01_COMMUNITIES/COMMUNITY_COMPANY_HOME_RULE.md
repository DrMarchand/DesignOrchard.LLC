## Rule

A Community can contain Companies as well as Homes.

A Company is an organized authority or operating entity inside a Community.
A Home is a stable place, address, or operating location inside a Community.

## Relationship

A Company may operate, own, govern, serve, or coordinate one or more Homes.
A Home may belong to, serve, host, or connect to one or more Companies.

## Updated Structural Model

Ecosystem -> Community -> Company
Ecosystem -> Community -> Home -> Package or Node or Record or Service

## Boundary Rules

A Company is not the same as a Home.
A Home is not automatically a Company.
A Community may contain both.

## Validation

Before a Home is assigned, Atlas should identify whether the Home belongs under a Company, serves a Company, or exists as a separate place inside the Community.
