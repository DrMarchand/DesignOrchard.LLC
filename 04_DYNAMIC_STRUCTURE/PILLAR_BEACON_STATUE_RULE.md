## Definitions

Pillar = foundational support.
Beacon = activated guidance signal.
Statue = durable symbolic enclosure for a proven beacon.

## Promotion Rule

A Pillar becomes a Beacon when its support value is proven enough to guide movement.
A Beacon becomes encased in a Statue when its guidance value becomes durable enough to represent identity, memory, and orientation.

No Beacon should be activated until the Pillar beneath it has proven support value.
No Statue should be created until the Beacon it encases has proven guidance value.

## Lifecycle

Idea -> Pillar -> Beacon -> Statue
Support -> Signal -> Embodiment
