## Definitions

Single Twin Mark = one visible face of a twin beacon system.
Paired Twin Mark = mirrored beacon relationship.
Twin Atom Twin Mark = paired beacon relationship bound by an atomic core.

## Dimensional Rule

On a one-dimensional plane, only one beacon is visible.
The mirrored counterpart may exist.
The atomic core may bind the structure.
The current view exposes only one face.

## Dimensional Reading

1D = one visible beacon
2D = mirrored beacon pair
3D = statue or embodied form

## Validation

When a single twin appears alone, treat it as the visible twin until Atlas confirms whether its mirror and core are present.

The full twin beacon system is not validated until Atlas can reconcile the visible signal, mirrored counterpart, and atomic center where applicable.
